Top 6 Must-watch Horror Animes

November 29, 2018

Anime is not only about interesting and adorable stories of love, family, and friendship, but it also exploits a variety of creepy, even frightening, adventures. Let’s find out six of the most obsessive animes that no horror movie fans should ignore!


Attack on Titan tells us a story of continuous battles among humans and Titans – giant and monstrous creatures fond of freshly killing and eating humans JUST FOR FUN instead of any survival demands. A minor number of humans alive choose to defend their last breath inside a city embraced by a super tall and solid wall. Interestingly, there is a group of people daring to get outside of the wall to fight against the Titans – the Scouting Legion. This horror anime is only about scary phenomena, but it's also going to teach us a lesson of intelligence and bravery.


Anime Yami Shibai was built upon a familiar idea regarding Japanese culture of horror story- telling, which sees numerous ancient houses and mysterious and dark streets. The content is concise, the art is simple, but sudden and creepy minor details in this anime can create an unforgettable obsession on the audience.
